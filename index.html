Features

Headset is a desktop music player built on top of YouTube and Reddit. Search through millions of songs and get all the recommendations, playlists, and songs that you need!

A library of millions.

YouTube is, without a doubt, the largest music library in the world. From massive hits to rare gems to cult classics, with more added every minute. It‚Äôs more music than you could listen to in a lifetime.

Music discovery done right.

Headset takes the songs that are shared in over 80 music subreddits, categorize them and plays them automatically. It's a great way to find new music and a pretty unique one as it is chosen by other humans like you, not by algorithms.

Make it yours.

Build a collection of your favorites, so they‚Äôre always close at hand. Save playlists and channels directly from YouTube, keep a "listen later" list of songs, or even capture hours of free MIT courses and TED talks. The possibilities are endless.

Want More?

Cross Platform

Headset is available for Windows, Linux (Debian, Redhat) and MacOs. You can even build it from source on a custom environment.

Themes

Choose between Dark and Light themes. Custom themes to come soon!

Last.fm

One click integration with the popular social music service. Scrobble all your songs and have the option to edit the information.

Private and Secure

All data, credentials, and cookies are transmitted over a secure SSL connection, to keep you invisible over the network.

Open Source

To ensure security and stability, a big part of Headset's source is kept open.

Cloud Syncing

Using multiple computers? No worries. Simply log in and get back to your music.
